Why You're Being Skipped

Completely Invisible to AI

While your competitors are being cited by ChatGPT and Claude, your site isn't even on the radar. Here's exactly why...

AI Bots Are Blocked

Most WordPress sites block GPTBot and ClaudeBot by default. If AI can't crawl you, it will never cite you.

Content Not AI-Structured

AI engines crave direct answers and FAQs. Most posts are written purely for humans — not for AI extraction.

No Answer Capsules

AI models prefer pages with extractable direct answers. Without them, you lose citations to competitors.

Missing llms.txt File

Early adopters using llms.txt report 40% more AI citations. Most sites don't have one — do you?

Thin Content Library

AI favours websites with deep, comprehensive content. Shallow posts are consistently skipped for citations.

No Author Entity Signals

Without E-E-A-T signals, AI treats your content as unverifiable. Named experts with credentials dramatically boost citation rates.
